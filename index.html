Frequently Asked

What is The Academia Group?

The Academia Group is a South Florida parent brand for two commonly-owned, vertically integrated companies: Academia Development Corp, a licensed Florida general contractor, and Academia Real Estate Corp, a licensed Florida real-estate brokerage. One platform handles a building's whole lifecycle — buy, build, recertify, restore, improve, protect, and sell.

Does Academia both build and broker?

Yes. Academia Real Estate Corp brokers the transaction and Academia Development Corp performs the construction, under common ownership — so a single operator can list, sell, renovate, recertify, and restore a South Florida property without handing it off between firms.

What licenses does Academia hold?

The companies hold, in-house, a Florida Certified General Contractor license (CGC1528882) and a Florida Real Estate Broker license (BK3384781), plus roofing (CCC1332624), HVAC (CAC1823051), plumbing (CFC1432695), electrical (EC13013134), and solar (CVC57202) trade licenses — each verifiable at MyFloridaLicense.com.

What areas does Academia serve?

Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach counties in South Florida.

What is SB-4D building recertification, and does Academia handle it?

Florida's SB-4D law requires milestone structural inspections (Florida Statute §553.899) and Structural Integrity Reserve Studies (SIRS) for condominium and cooperative buildings — the milestone inspection is due by year 30, or year 25 where the local enforcement agency requires it. Academia Development Corp handles the full path: milestone inspection support, SIRS-driven capital planning, and the concrete restoration, waterproofing, roofing, and MEP repairs the reports require.
